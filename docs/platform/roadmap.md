# Roadmap

Portfolio planning in **Roadmap**, **Kanban**, and **Timeline** views.

:::note
**Preview** — Roadmap is not enabled for all workspaces yet. If you do not see it in the sidebar, it is not available on yours.
:::

---

## How to access

**Preview > Roadmap** in the left sidebar (when enabled).

---

## What you can do

- Switch between Roadmap, Kanban, and Timeline views
- Filter by owner, status, tags, and kind
- Show or hide done and archived projects
- Open a project for specs and planning
