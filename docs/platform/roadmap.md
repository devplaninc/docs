# Roadmap

Portfolio planning across releases, phases, and delivery timelines. **Roadmap** gives you three views — Roadmap, Kanban, and Timeline — to visualize and manage your project portfolio.

---

## How to access

Click **Roadmap** under the **Specs** section in the left sidebar.

:::note
Roadmap is only visible when specs are enabled for your workspace.
:::

---

## Key features

- **Three view modes** — **Roadmap** (grouped columns), **Kanban** (status columns), and **Timeline** (phase-based timeline)
- **Rich filters** — search, tags, kind, status, owner, and view-by dimension
- **Show/hide done and archived** — control which projects appear
- **Column customization** — adjust which columns are visible in each view
- **Persisted filters** — filter state is saved so you return to the same view
- **Project navigation** — click a project to open its detail page, with return-to-roadmap context

---

## Common workflows

1. Open **Roadmap** to see your project portfolio at a glance.
2. Switch between **Roadmap**, **Kanban**, and **Timeline** depending on what you need.
3. Apply filters to focus on a specific owner, status, or tag.
4. Toggle done and archived projects on or off.
5. Click a project to open its detail page for specs and planning.
