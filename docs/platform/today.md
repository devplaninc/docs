# Today

AI-generated daily digest of what's changing in your workspace. Default landing page when you open Devplan.

---

## What you can do

- Toggle **Workspace** vs **Personal** digest
- Browse previous digests
- **Personalize** your personal digest — tone, focus, formatting; click **Generate** for an on-demand email
- Click **evidence pills** (`3 sources`) to open linked PRs, tickets, Slack threads, and docs

Personal digest email and delivery: [Preferences](/preferences). Workspace-wide digest settings: [Workspace Daily Digest](/workspace-daily-digest) (Admins).
