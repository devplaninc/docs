# Updates

A pulse feed of what shipped recently in your product catalog. **Updates** groups change reports by day so you can see what moved across your product areas.

---

## How to access

Click **Updates** in the left sidebar.

---

## Key features

- **What shipped recently** — headline view of catalog changes over time
- **Product area filters** — pill filters with counts for each product area (e.g. Integrations & APIs, Planning & Roadmaps)
- **Search** — find specific updates by keyword
- **Day-grouped feed** — updates organized by date with entry counts
- **Date timeline** — right sidebar for jumping to a specific day or week
- **Report detail** — click any update to open the full change report

---

## Common workflows

1. Open **Updates** to scan what changed recently.
2. Filter by product area to focus on a specific part of your product.
3. Use search to find updates mentioning a specific feature or topic.
4. Click an update to read the full report with linked sources.
5. Use the date timeline to jump to a specific day.
