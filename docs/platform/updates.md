# Updates

**Updates** is the change history of your product — a chronological, filterable timeline of what
changed in your [feature catalog](/knowledge#product-catalog), grouped by day and traceable back
to the source.

## Why it matters

Decisions and changes get scattered across tools and lost over time. Updates keeps a single,
durable record: every entry preserves its context and links back to the PRs, tickets, and
discussions behind it. When you need to know *what changed, when, and why* — for a stakeholder
update, an incident review, or just catching up after time away — it's all here.

## What you can do

- Filter by product area or search by keyword
- Jump to a specific date using the timeline sidebar
- Open any report for the full detail and its linked sources
