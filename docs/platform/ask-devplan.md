# Ask Devplan

Conversational AI scoped to your workspace. Ask about product changes, blockers, and decisions — answers cite connected sources.

---

## What you can do

- Chat in natural language; attach files for extra context
- Use suggested prompts or start a **New chat**
- Search and rename past chats in the left rail
- Click **evidence pills** in responses to inspect source references
