# Insights

Synthesized, role-aware takeaways from your workspace signals. **Insights** distills raw evidence from connected sources into prioritized briefs you can act on.

---

## How to access

Click **Insights** in the left sidebar.

:::note
If you don't see Insights in the sidebar, contact your workspace admin.
:::

---

## Key features

- **Insight cards** — each card shows a headline, description, and kind tags (Risk, Problem / opportunity, Feedback theme)
- **Signal and source counts** — see how much evidence backs each insight
- **Evidence pills** — click to open a flyout listing the sources behind an insight (see below)
- **Read brief** — open a separate flyout with the full insight narrative and related signals
- **Search and filter** — find insights by keyword or kind
- **Sort** — order by newest first or other criteria
- **Role-based relevance** — insights are filtered based on your role in profile preferences

---

## Evidence pills

Each insight card shows an evidence pill (e.g. `5 sources`) next to the signal count when sources are available. The pill displays stacked icons for the integrations involved — GitHub, Slack, Jira, Confluence, and others.

Click the evidence pill to open a **Sources** flyout on the right side of the screen. The flyout lists each reference backing the insight:

- **Source icons and rationale** — see which integration produced each reference and the note explaining its relevance
- **Origin links** — open the original PR, Slack thread, ticket, or document
- **Details toggle** — expand a row to read captured content, metadata, and linked artifacts

:::note
**Evidence pills** and **Read brief** are different. The evidence pill shows the raw source references. **Read brief** opens the synthesized insight narrative and its related signals.
:::

---

## Common workflows

1. Open **Insights** to scan prioritized takeaways for your role.
2. Filter by kind (e.g. Risk, Feedback theme) to focus on what matters most.
3. Click an **evidence pill** to inspect the sources behind an insight.
4. Click **Read brief** to read the full insight narrative and related signals.
5. Adjust your role in **Settings > Profile > Preferences** to change which insights surface for you. See [Preferences](/settings/preferences) for details.
6. Follow links to related signals for the raw evidence behind an insight.
