# Product

Browse and refine the auto-generated feature catalogue for your product. **Product** shows what Devplan knows about each feature — user flows, technical design, and implementation status — organized by product area.

---

## How to access

Click **Product** under the **Knowledge** section in the left sidebar.

---

## Key features

- **Feature catalogue** — features grouped by product area in the left panel
- **Search** — find features by name across all areas
- **Spec viewer** — read feature specs with **User Flow** and **Tech Design** tabs
- **Status badges** — see implementation status (Implemented, In Progress, Planned, etc.)
- **On-page navigation** — table of contents for long specs
- **Refine section** — submit feedback to improve a catalogue section

---

## Common workflows

1. Open **Product** to explore what Devplan knows about your product.
2. Browse by product area in the left panel, or search for a specific feature.
3. Click a feature to read its user flow and technical design.
4. Use the on-page table of contents to jump to a specific section.
5. Click **Refine section** to submit corrections or additions to a spec.
