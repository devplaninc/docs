# Signals

The raw evidence feed from your connected sources. **Signals** shows the individual data points — from code, tickets, docs, Slack, and more — that Devplan synthesizes into [Insights](/insights).

---

## How to access

Click **Signals** under the **Knowledge** section in the left sidebar.

:::note
If you don't see Signals in the sidebar, contact your workspace admin.
:::

---

## Key features

- **Signal feed** — chronological list of evidence from connected integrations
- **Source tags** — each signal shows which integration and content type produced it
- **Search** — find signals by keyword
- **Source-type filters** — filter by GitHub, Slack, Jira, Confluence, and other sources
- **Sort** — order by newest first
- **Evidence flyout** — inspect the full artifact behind a signal
- **Links to Insights** — jump to synthesized takeaways related to a signal

---

## Common workflows

1. Open **Signals** to browse incoming evidence from your connected tools.
2. Filter by source type to focus on a specific integration (e.g. Slack threads, GitHub PRs).
3. Search for signals related to a feature, project, or topic.
4. Click a signal to open the evidence flyout and read the full context.
5. Follow links to **Insights** for synthesized takeaways based on related signals.
