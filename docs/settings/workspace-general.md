# Workspace General

Manage core workspace identity — name, logo, and website — and access destructive actions like workspace deletion.

---

## How to access

Open **Settings** from your profile menu, then click **General** under **Workspace**.

---

## Key features

- **Workspace name** — the display name shown in the sidebar and company switcher
- **Logo** — upload a workspace icon
- **Website** — the URL Devplan uses when scanning your product for context (also editable under [Workspace](/workspace) in the app)
- **Danger Zone** — delete the workspace (collapsed by default)

---

## Common workflows

### Update workspace details

1. Open **Settings > Workspace > General**.
2. Edit the name, upload a logo, or update the website URL.
3. Save your changes.

### Delete a workspace

1. Expand **Danger Zone** at the bottom of the page.
2. Review the deletion warning.
3. Confirm to permanently delete the workspace and its data.

:::note
Deleting a workspace is irreversible. Only workspace owners should perform this action.
:::
