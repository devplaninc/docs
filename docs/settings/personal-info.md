# Personal Info

Update the personal details associated with your Devplan account and manage your workspace memberships.

---

## How to access

Open **Settings** from your profile menu, then click **Personal Info** under **Profile**.

---

## Key features

- **Email** — view the email address associated with your account (read-only)
- **Full name** — edit your display name used across the app
- **Workspace invites** — review and accept or decline pending invitations
- **Active workspaces** — see workspaces you belong to and leave a workspace if needed

---

## Common workflows

### Update your name

1. Open **Settings > Profile > Personal Info**.
2. Edit **Full Name**.
3. Click **Update profile**.

### Accept a workspace invite

1. Open **Personal Info** — pending invites appear at the top when available.
2. Review the workspace name and role.
3. Click **Accept** to join or **Decline** to dismiss the invite.

### Leave a workspace

1. Scroll to **Active workspaces**.
2. Find the workspace you want to leave.
3. Use the actions menu and confirm **Leave workspace**.
