# Preferences

Personalize how Devplan works for you across workspaces — from stakeholder roles and appearance to Daily Digest email delivery.

---

## How to access

Open **Settings** from your profile menu, then click **Preferences** under **Profile**.

---

## Key features

### Select your role

Pick the stakeholder roles you care about (e.g. Product Manager, Engineering Lead). Devplan uses these selections to personalize content like [Insights](/platform/insights). Selections save automatically.

Leave all roles unchecked for the default experience.

### AI coding

When specs are enabled in any of your workspaces:

- **Preferred IDE** — choose your editor (Cursor, VS Code, etc.). Devplan uses this to personalize CLI flags.

### Daily Digest

Control whether you receive Daily Digest emails for each workspace you belong to. Toggle the **Email** switch per workspace.

This is the same setting linked from **Manage communication settings** on the [Today](/platform/today) personalize panel.

### Appearance

- **Color theme** — choose **Light**, **Dark**, or **System**. Saved automatically for your browser.

---

## Common workflows

1. Open **Preferences** and select the roles relevant to your work.
2. Set your preferred IDE if you use the Devplan CLI.
3. Toggle Daily Digest email delivery on or off per workspace.
4. Choose a color theme that suits your environment.
