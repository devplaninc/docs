# Workspace Members

Invite teammates, manage roles, and review pending invitations for your workspace.

---

## How to access

Open **Settings** from your profile menu, then click **Members** under **Workspace**.

---

## Key features

- **Invite members** — send email invitations with a chosen role
- **Active members table** — view all members with name, email, and role
- **Role management** — change a member's role inline (Admin or User)
- **Remove members** — remove a member from the workspace
- **Pending invites** — view and revoke outstanding invitations

---

## Common workflows

### Invite a new member

1. Open **Settings > Workspace > Members**.
2. Click **Invite**.
3. Enter the member's email and select a role (**Admin** or **User** by default).
4. Click **Invite** to send the invitation.

The invitee can accept from **Settings > Profile > Personal Info** or via the invitation email.

### Change a member's role

1. Find the member in the **Active members** table.
2. Use the **Role** dropdown to select a new role.
3. Changes take effect immediately.

### Revoke a pending invite

1. Review the pending invites section.
2. Use the actions menu on the invite to revoke it.
