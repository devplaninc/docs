# Organization General

Manage your organization's identity, owners, and destructive actions.

---

## How to access

Open **Settings** from your profile menu, then click **General** under **Organization**.

:::note
Organization settings are only visible when you belong to an organization.
:::

---

## Key features

- **Organization name** — the display name for your organization
- **Logo** — upload an organization icon
- **Organization owners** — view and manage users with owner-level access across the org
- **Danger Zone** — delete the organization (requires no active subscription and no workspaces)

---

## Common workflows

### Update organization details

1. Open **Settings > Organization > General**.
2. Edit the name or upload a logo.
3. Save your changes.

### Manage organization owners

1. Review the owners table under **Organization owners**.
2. Use **Add owner** to grant owner access to another user.
3. Use the remove action to revoke owner access (you cannot remove yourself).

### Delete an organization

1. Expand **Danger Zone**.
2. Confirm the organization has no active subscription and no workspaces.
3. Complete the deletion flow.

:::note
Deleting an organization is irreversible.
:::
