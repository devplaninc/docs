# Settings Overview

Open **Settings** from your profile menu in the bottom-left corner of the app. Settings are organized into three groups that mirror the sidebar: **Profile**, **Workspace**, and **Organization**.

Use **Back to app** at the top of the settings sidebar to return to your workspace.

---

## Profile

Account-level settings that follow you across workspaces.

| Page | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| [Personal Info](/settings/personal-info) | Update your name, email, and workspace memberships |
| [Preferences](/settings/preferences) | Roles, appearance, IDE, and Daily Digest email delivery |

---

## Workspace

Settings scoped to the current workspace.

| Page | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| [General](/settings/workspace-general) | Workspace name, logo, website, and deletion |
| [Members](/settings/workspace-members) | Invite members, manage roles, and pending invites |
| [Daily Digest](/settings/workspace-daily-digest) | Workspace-wide digest instructions, delivery, and Slack |
| [API Keys](/settings/workspace-api-keys) | Create and manage workspace API keys for MCP access |

:::note
**Daily Digest** and **API Keys** may not appear for all workspaces depending on your plan.
:::

---

## Organization

Settings for managing an organization and its workspaces. Visible when you belong to an organization.

| Page | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| [General](/settings/organization-general) | Organization name, logo, owners, and deletion |
| [Workspaces](/settings/organization-workspaces) | Create, add, and manage workspaces in the org |
| [Billing](/settings/organization-billing) | Enterprise plan and subscription management |
