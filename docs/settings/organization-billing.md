# Organization Billing

Manage your organization's enterprise subscription and billing through Stripe.

---

## How to access

Open **Settings** from your profile menu, then click **Billing** under **Organization**.

:::note
Organization settings are only visible when you belong to an organization.
:::

---

## Key features

- **Enterprise plan cards** — view and select an enterprise plan when no active subscription exists
- **Manage Enterprise Subscription** — open the Stripe customer portal to update payment method, view invoices, or change plan
- **Subscription status** — see whether the organization has an active enterprise subscription

An active enterprise subscription is required for [Organization Workspaces](/settings/organization-workspaces) management.

---

## Common workflows

### Enroll in an enterprise plan

1. Open **Settings > Organization > Billing**.
2. Review the available enterprise plan cards.
3. Select a plan and complete checkout through Stripe.

### Manage an existing subscription

1. Open **Organization > Billing**.
2. Click **Manage Enterprise Subscription**.
3. Use the Stripe portal to update payment details, view invoices, or modify your plan.

### Enable workspace management

After enrolling, return to [Organization Workspaces](/settings/organization-workspaces) to create or add workspaces.
