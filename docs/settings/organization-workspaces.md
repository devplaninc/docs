# Organization Workspaces

Create, add, and manage workspaces within your organization.

---

## How to access

Open **Settings** from your profile menu, then click **Workspaces** under **Organization**.

:::note
Organization settings are only visible when you belong to an organization. Workspace management requires an active enterprise subscription.
:::

---

## Key features

- **Workspaces table** — view all workspaces in the organization
- **Create Workspace** — create a new workspace under the organization
- **Add Workspace** — link an existing standalone workspace to the organization
- **Workspace actions** — manage individual workspaces from the table

---

## Common workflows

### Create a new workspace

1. Open **Settings > Organization > Workspaces**.
2. Click **Create Workspace**.
3. Enter workspace details and confirm.
4. The new workspace appears in the table and in your workspace switcher.

### Add an existing workspace

1. Click **Add Workspace**.
2. Select a workspace you own that is not yet part of the organization.
3. Confirm to link it under the org.

### Enroll in enterprise billing first

If workspace management is disabled, enroll your organization in an enterprise plan from [Organization Billing](/settings/organization-billing) first.
