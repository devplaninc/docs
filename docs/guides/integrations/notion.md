# Notion

Attach pages or databases for on-demand reads during signal processing.

---

## Connect and attach

1. **Knowledge > Integrations** → **Notion** → **Connect Notion**
2. Click **Attach Notion content** and select pages or databases

Devplan reads attached content on demand — not your entire Notion workspace. Remove attachments from the manage table anytime.
