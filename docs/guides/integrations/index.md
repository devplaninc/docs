# Integrations

Connect the tools your team already uses so Devplan can learn about your product, generate [Signals](/signals) and [Insights](/insights), build your feature catalogue, and ground project planning in real context.

Open **Knowledge > Integrations** in the sidebar to connect and manage sources.

---

## After connecting

1. Complete any **Configure**, **Select folders**, or **Attach** steps for the provider — connecting an account alone is often not enough.
2. Allow time for background processing. Repository analysis and catalogue bootstrap typically take 30 minutes or longer; signals and insights grow over hours and days. See [Core Workflow](/core-workflow) for timelines.
3. Check [Signals](/signals), [Insights](/insights), and [Today](/today) as data starts flowing in.
